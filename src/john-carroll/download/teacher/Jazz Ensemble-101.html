Jazz Ensemble 16-17

This is a graded class. Attendance is required. Do not schedule make-up tests, teacher meetings etc during jazz time.
No late entry without a pass.
Dress code is still in effect.

There will be tests, quizzes, and other graded work.
Performance will be a large part of your grade. Performance includes: in-class playing/preparedness, class participation, Open House (Oct 22), Winter concert (Dec 9), Spring concert (Apr 12), and other performances. Every performance is a group project.

We will use OneNote. If you do not have the latest version installed, you may need to use OneNote via the web browser or update your current version via your school account. See me if you need assistance.

You must obtain a copy of The Real Book version 6 in your appropriate key no later than September 11, 2016. Percussionists, you should probably get a C version unless you play another instrument.

You will record yourself playing.
You will be expected to sing melodies and rhythms in class.

General daily schedule:
Prayer
Metronome exercise
Theory
Performance piece practice or other tunes from The Real Book or other sources.
